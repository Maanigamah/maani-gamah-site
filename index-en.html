About Us

About Maʻani Al-Qimmah Logistics Services

Maʻani Al-Qimmah Logistics Services

We are Saudi professionals specialized in providing integrated logistics services with our partners to facilitate logistics operations for our clients, starting from sourcing high-quality products and factories, completing import procedures, following up manufacturing, ensuring product quality before shipping to Saudi Arabia, completing customs clearance procedures, receiving the goods, and transporting them to the client’s location.
